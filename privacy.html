Privacy Policy

Plain-language privacy information for Games Done Correctly.

This page explains how Games Done Correctly handles information for GamesDoneCorrectly.com and our games, including Idle Trader. We use Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics to collect limited crash, diagnostic, testing, and gameplay telemetry that helps us investigate bugs and improve quality.

At a glance

  • We collect limited technical and testing information through Firebase.
  • Crash reports may include app version, device context, and in-game debugging state.
  • We use this information to diagnose issues, improve stability, and support QA.
  • We do not sell crash or testing data.

1. Scope

This policy covers our website and our games unless a product has its own notice.

This Privacy Policy describes how Games Done Correctly ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you visit GamesDoneCorrectly.com or use one of our games, including Idle Trader. If a specific product later publishes a separate privacy notice, that more specific notice will control for that product.

2. Collection

We currently collect limited technical, crash, and testing information through Firebase.

  • Crash reports, stack traces, and diagnostic logs used to investigate errors.
  • Technical details such as app version, operating system version, timestamps, and device context.
  • In-game debugging context such as current screen, save slot identifier, ship type, planet, tutorial state, combat state, and recent diagnostic messages when needed to reproduce a bug.
  • Testing and analytics events such as session starts, screen views, tutorial progress, travel events, combat outcomes, ship changes, beta overrides, and developer tool usage.

We do not currently use this site to collect payment card data, mailing-address information, or customer account registration details.

3. Use

We use collected information to improve stability, test features, and fix bugs faster.

  • Detect, log, and diagnose crashes or technical issues.
  • Reproduce bug reports and understand what happened when a problem occurred.
  • Measure whether screens, tutorials, and beta features are behaving as expected.
  • Prioritize fixes and improve reliability.
  • Maintain the security and operation of our website and games.

4. Firebase And Google

Firebase acts as our service provider for crash reporting and analytics.

We use Google Firebase services, including Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics, to process technical and testing information on our behalf. Information sent to Firebase is also subject to Google’s privacy and security practices.

Learn more at Firebase Privacy and Security and Google Privacy Policy.

5. Sharing

We share information only where needed to operate our services or comply with law.

  • With service providers such as Firebase or hosting providers that help operate and improve the service.
  • If required by law, legal process, or a good-faith belief that disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, or service integrity.
  • As part of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to applicable law.

We do not sell, rent, or trade crash, testing, or diagnostic information as a product.

6. Choices

You can stop future app data collection by stopping use of the app or uninstalling it.

Because the Firebase information we collect is tied to quality assurance, debugging, and service reliability, an in-app opt-out may not currently be available for all diagnostic events. If you no longer want the app to send future crash or analytics information, the simplest option is to stop using the app and uninstall it.

7. Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary for debugging and improvement.

We retain crash, diagnostic, and testing information for as long as reasonably needed to investigate issues, understand product health, improve quality, meet legal obligations, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods may also depend on controls available through Firebase and other service providers.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to our privacy contact.

Email: privacy@gamesdonecorrectly.com

If you want a different privacy contact later, this page can be updated at any time.
