Privacy Policy for Games Done Correctly

This Privacy Policy explains how Games Done Correctly ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our games, including Idle Trader.

Our apps use Google Firebase services, including Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics, to collect technical and usage data automatically. This information helps us monitor performance, fix issues, and improve the game.

1. Information Collected

Information is collected automatically through Firebase and may include:

  • Crash reports, stack traces, and diagnostic data
  • Device information such as device model, operating system, and app version
  • General usage data such as session activity, screens viewed, and gameplay events
  • Approximate location based on IP address (as provided by Firebase)

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or payment details within the app.

2. How Information Is Used

Collected information is used to:

  • Identify and fix crashes or technical issues
  • Improve gameplay balance and performance
  • Understand general usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on third-party services to operate and improve our apps. These include:

  • Google Firebase (Crashlytics and Analytics)
  • Advertising services (which may be added or used in the future)

These services may collect, process, and store data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

Information collected through third-party services is shared with those providers as part of their operation. We do not sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained and managed according to the policies and settings of the third-party services we use, such as Firebase.

6. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age required by applicable law, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
