1. Scope

This policy covers our website and our games unless a title has its own separate notice.

This Privacy Policy explains how Games Done Correctly ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you visit GamesDoneCorrectly.com or use one of our games, including Idle Trader. If we publish a game, beta, or service with a separate privacy policy, that more specific policy will control for that product.

Our goal is simple: collect the minimum technical information needed to monitor crashes, improve testing, investigate bugs, and keep the product working well.

2. What We Collect

We currently collect limited technical, crash, and testing information through Firebase.

Depending on how the app is used, the information we collect may include:

  • Crash reports, stack traces, and diagnostic logs used to investigate errors.
  • Device and technical information such as app version, operating system version, device type, and timestamps.
  • In-app debugging context such as the current screen, save slot identifier, ship type, planet, tutorial state, combat state, and recent diagnostic messages when needed to reproduce a bug.
  • Testing and analytics events such as session starts, screen views, tutorial progress, travel events, combat outcomes, ship changes, beta overrides, and developer tool usage.
  • Basic website-delivery data that a hosting provider may create automatically, such as routine server or network logs required to serve the page securely.

We do not currently use this site to collect account registrations, payment card information, mailing addresses, or similar direct customer profile data.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to improve stability, test features, and fix bugs faster.

We may use the information described above to:

  • Detect, log, and diagnose crashes and other technical issues.
  • Reproduce bug reports and understand what was happening inside the game when a problem occurred.
  • Measure whether tutorials, screens, and beta features are functioning as expected.
  • Prioritize fixes, improve balancing, and make future releases more reliable.
  • Maintain the security, integrity, and basic operation of our website and games.

We do not use this Firebase data to build advertising profiles, and we do not sell it.

4. Firebase And Google

Firebase acts as our service provider for crash reporting and analytics.

We use Google Firebase services, including Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics, to process technical and testing information on our behalf. Information sent to Firebase is also subject to Google’s privacy and security practices.

You can learn more here: Firebase Privacy and Security and Google Privacy Policy.

5. Sharing And Disclosure

We share information only where needed to operate our services or comply with law.

We may share or disclose information in the following limited situations:

  • With service providers such as Firebase or website-hosting providers that help us operate, secure, analyze, or improve the service.
  • If required by law, legal process, or a good-faith belief that disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the service.
  • As part of a business transfer such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to applicable law.

We do not sell, rent, or trade crash, testing, or diagnostic information as a product.

6. Your Choices

You can stop future app data collection by stopping use of the app or uninstalling it.

Because the Firebase information we collect is tied to quality assurance, debugging, and service reliability, an in-app opt-out may not currently be available for all diagnostic events. If you no longer want the app to send future crash or analytics information, the most effective option is to stop using the app and uninstall it.

If you contact us about privacy, please include the app name, platform, and approximate timeframe so we can understand your question more quickly.

7. Retention

We keep information for as long as it is reasonably needed for debugging and service improvement.

We retain crash, diagnostic, and testing information for as long as reasonably necessary to investigate issues, understand product health, improve game quality, meet legal obligations, and enforce our agreements. Retention periods may also depend on the settings and controls available through Firebase and other service providers.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time.

If we make material changes, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the "Updated" date at the top. Continued use of the website or app after an update means the revised policy will apply going forward.
